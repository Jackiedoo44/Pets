About Us

Paw Goods is a specialty store for people who truly cherish their dogs and have the same desire for excellent service and products for their pets as we do.Our goal is to provide a unique and wonderful experience to all of our clients on a daily basis. And to pet all the dogs, of course.We are a family-owned dog training and supplies company dedicated to providing and supplying dog items like Tre Ponti Dog Harnesses, Cricket Dog Treats, Doggie Chicken Chips, and DogSheetz just to name a few. We have been in the business of providing quality dog-related products ever since we started back in 2021.
We are proud to say that we are a company known to grant high-quality dog-related products as well as dog-training services.
As one of the leading companies, we value our clients’ opinions, recommendations and suggestions about the services that we provide. For that, we are always open to accepting new ideas and ventures that our customers want us to offer next. We take it a point to always listen to our clients for to us satisfy their whims and needs is the first and foremost thing that we should do. So, we encourage our clients to never hesitate to contact us.If you are looking for quality dog products, then you are looking at the right direction for we are the company that will always be there to cater all your pet needs.
We are the company that you can trust for your dog’s needs. We promise to deliver you the kind of service that you and your dog truly deserve.
